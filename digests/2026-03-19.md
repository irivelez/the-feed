The agentic AI stack is crystallizing fast — coding agents, security agents, browser agents, enterprise runtimes — but the bottleneck has flipped from capability to deployment: governance, reliability, and trust are now what separates demos from production.

- The capability-to-deployment gap is real: enterprises stall on governance, observability, and human-in-the-loop — not on model quality
